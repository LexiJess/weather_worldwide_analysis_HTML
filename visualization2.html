Latitude versus Humidity

Latitude versus humidity does not show a clear pattern of correlation. Unlike maximum temperature, humidity does not tend to cluster around the equator. This, too, makes intuitive sense. Many equatorial regions are dry deserts OR wet jungles. Many non-equatorial regions are humid costal zones or arid polar regions.
